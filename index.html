About

Hello! I'm Leo Lee, a student based in Xiamen, Fujian, China.

I'm a devoted Arch Linux user. I appreciate the control that comes from building a system from the ground up—minimal, flexible, and always cutting-edge. Between the terminal and code, I find my joy.

Beyond technology, photography is how I document life. I have a particular fondness for Panasonic cameras, especially their Real Time LUT feature. This "what you see is what you get" experience allows me to bake the creative vision directly into the photo at the moment of capture, eliminating tedious post-processing and delivering the texture I desire straight away.
